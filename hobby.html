My Hobbies

Electronics and Microchips

One of my primary hobbies is delving into the world of electronics, especially microchips. I enjoy understanding how they work, from their basic logic gates to complex architectures. I spend time experimenting with microcontrollers like Arduino and Raspberry Pi, building small circuits, and learning about embedded systems programming.

This fascination extends to reading about the latest advancements in semiconductor technology and trying to grasp the intricate details of chip design. It's a challenging yet rewarding hobby that perfectly complements my interest in computer engineering.

Reading and Learning

When I'm not tinkering with electronics, I'm usually found with a book or an online course. I have a broad interest in non-fiction, particularly in areas like history, science, and philosophy. I believe that continuous learning is key to personal and professional growth. I also enjoy reading about new programming languages and software development methodologies.

I also enjoy reading fiction, especially sci-fi and fantasy, as a way to relax and explore imaginative worlds. It's a great way to unwind and get inspired.
